Workshops Session 1

How to Set Up the 99F Sound System

Hosted by Philip Tan & Don Derek Haddad        3rd Floor Atrium

We will show how we transport, set up, test, and pack up the Media Lab's 99F sound system! If we have time, we may also show how we set up the lights.

Drawing Humans: Character Concept Art

Hosted by Jocelyn Shen        E14-240

Crash course in character concept sketching. Topics that will be covered (in rapid fire) are tentatively conveying emotion, drawing the human head (portraits), drawing the human figure, costumes and clothing, shape (2D), and form (3D). Participants will follow along with drawing instructions, discuss character ideas in groups, and sketch their own characters.

Chunky Hand Knitting a Bag!

Hosted by Kyung Yun Choi (Yun)        3rd Floor Atrium

It will be a cozy and chill knitting session using your arms instead of knitting needle :) I'm thinking of hosting a short session for knitting a bowl or tote bag hand-knitted with a chunky velvet yarn or it could be more like crafting something fun with hand-knitting. Due to the material cost, we might need to hold this with limited number of people so better to have a registration form. Preregistration required

1:30-3:30pm
Workshops Session 2

Nootropics

Hosted by Nathan Whitmore        E14-240

Nootropics are supplements and drugs that improve cognition! We'll go over the types, how they work, and how you can buy them! We'll also have some (safe) samples and cognitive tests to try!

HONK! Media Lab & Parade!

Hosted by Sean Hickey & Lily Gabaree        E14-240

In the spirit of the HONK! brass band festival that happens every year in Somerville, we'll bring folks together to learn one or two brass band tunes over the first hour (no experience necessary) and then we'll go on a parade around the lab playing those tunes. We'll have a few folks on hand to support and teach parts and (hopefully) extra instruments for those who don't have one. Folks are encouraged to bring their own horn, reed, or percussion instruments if they have one.
